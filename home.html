Pups & Cups is a dog cafe inspired by dog cafes in Asia and we want to bring this culture to New York! Behold, an environment where food lovers and dog lovers can meet up.

Why Pups & Cups?



Tasteful


Our foods are made by our highly-trained chefs with utmost care. We focus on providing high quality food on a daily basis.


Healthful


Fresh, all-organic ingredients, because we believe that health is wealth. Who said tasty doesn't mean healthy?


Dogful


No shortage of playful dogs here. You'll always find our own dogs at the cafe, plus other dogs from our patrons!
